About Qasr-e-Zaiqa

A Legacy of Flavor

Qasr-e-Zaiqa, meaning "Palace of Taste," is Chakwal's premier destination for authentic Pakistani cuisine. Established in 2010, we've been serving exquisite dishes that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Pakistan.

Our restaurant features three distinct dining experiences: the elegant Family Area, the traditional Gents Section, and our beautiful Lawn Dining area surrounded by lush gardens.

Every dish at Qasr-e-Zaiqa is prepared with the finest ingredients, traditional recipes, and a passion for perfection that has made us a landmark in Chakwal's culinary scene.
